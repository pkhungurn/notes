Some tables and graphs from the paper.

Flow matching models beat baseline diffusion models in unconditional image generation.

The metrics are

  • NLL = negative log likelihood
  • FID = Frechet Inception Distance
  • NFE = number of function evaluations until reach a certain quality (not sure about this)

Flow matching models train faster.
